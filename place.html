Place to go in Surabaya

Discover the authentic place of Surabaya

Tunjungan Plaza

Tunjungan Plaza is a large, integrated mixed-use complex and shopping mall in Surabaya, Indonesia, containing retail outlets, restaurants, offices, and hotels. Known as the first modern shopping center in the city, it is divided into several buildings (TP1 through TP6) and is a significant landmark, housing numerous brands and being the second-largest mall in Surabaya.

KBS

Surabaya Zoo is one of Indonesia's most comprehensive zoos and was once the most comprehensive in Southeast Asia, with over 211 different species and over 2,236 animals. Surabaya Zoo also serves as a conservation, educational, research, and recreational facility.

Masjid Al-Akbar

Al-Akbar Grand Mosque is the second largest mosque in Indonesia after the Istiqlal mosque in Jakarta. Located in the south of Surabaya, Al-Akbar mosque is an attraction for tourists visiting Surabaya. The mosque with a capacity of 30000 worshippers was inaugurated on November 10, 2000. In addition, there is also Urban Farming, Edu Park and the tower of the mosque which is open to the public.

Jl. Tunjungan

Jl.Tunjungan is a major and iconic street in Downtown Surabaya, Indonesia, known for its historical significance, vibrant atmosphere, and being a central hub for shops, cafes, restaurants, and hotels, including the legendary Hotel Majapahit.
