Get to know more about Me! 😼

Hi, I’m Rara! ✨

Bonjour! My name is Nasyita Larasati Ertyananda, but everyone calls me Rara 🤩. I’m from Surabaya 🌆 and currently a second-year Informatics Engineering student at Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember 🖥️📚. I’ve always been curious about technology and love learning new things—whether it’s coding a small project, exploring digital tools, or just reading about the latest tech trends.

When it comes to hobbies, I’m a huge movie fan 🎬. I especially enjoy films with a sad ending because I feel like they really make you reflect on life and emotions. Alongside movies, I spend a lot of time listening to music, mostly emotional, heart-touching songs 🎶 that match my dramatic moods. Another hobby I adore is drawing everyday objects around me ✏️🖼️—from the coffee cup on my desk ☕ to gadgets I use daily. It’s a relaxing way to observe life, get creative, and notice small details that often go unnoticed.

And of course… I’m a huge cat lover 🐱💖! I can spend hours cuddling with cats, watching funny or cute cat videos, or even imagining silly cat adventures. They always make my day brighter and my heart happier 😺✨.
